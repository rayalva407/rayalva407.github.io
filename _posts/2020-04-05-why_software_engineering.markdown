When I first discovered coding it was one of the most frustrating things I have tried to learn yet it always kept me coming back. No matter how hard it was it always felt rewarding at the end of the day. Coding is a challenge that fulfills my need for knowledge. That is why I chose software engineering.  Everyday there is always something new to learn. I have an eagerness to explore, to learn and to create. There is something inspiring about how technology is changing the world and shaping our society. Technology is part of the real world and the thought that someday I will be able to create something that could potentially ignite social change, interrupt industries or help people is exhilarating. I am ready for a change in my life. Software engineering brings endless possibilities. The knowledge that it will bring will be useful in all parts of my life. Software engineering is an adventure that will never end.
